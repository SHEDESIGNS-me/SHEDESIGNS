Born to Create: Embracing the Journey of a Graphic Designer

From an early age, the spark of creativity ignites within some of us, leaving an indelible mark on our souls. The pursuit of creativity, like a compass guiding our path, leads us to the vibrant world of graphic design. Whether it's sketching doodles on school notebooks or experimenting with digital design tools, graphic designers are driven by an innate desire to transform ideas into visual masterpieces. Residing in the heart of Brooklyn, NY, I draw inspiration from the diverse culture and bustling energy of the city. Each day, I find myself enchanted by the endless possibilities that design offers. Whether it's crafting a captivating logo, bringing characters to life through animation, or creating stunning illustrations that evoke emotions, I pour my heart and soul into every project. My artistic journey began with a spark of curiosity and a love for self-expression. After a hiatus from the world of design, I found myself returning to my true calling - the world of visual storytelling. The decision to rekindle my creative flame led me to pursue a major in Internet/Digital Media Design at EGCC, where I immersed myself in a world of artistic exploration. I am Sherese Maynard, the one-woman powerhouse driving the magic behind this blog. As a Graphic Designer, Illustrator, Animator, and Front-end Developer based in the vibrant city of New York, I am a multidisciplinary artist with a burning passion for all things creative. What's New In Design:Exploring the Exciting Trends and Innovations

Augmented Reality (AR) and Immersive Experiences:
Design is a dynamic and ever-evolving field, constantly influenced by technological advancements, cultural shifts, and the creative spirit of designers worldwide. As we embark on a journey to discover "What's New in Design," we find ourselves at the cusp of exciting innovations and trends that redefine the way we perceive and interact with visual communication. Join me as we explore the cutting-edge concepts and fresh ideas that are shaping the future of design
One of the most thrilling developments in design is the integration of Augmented Reality (AR) and immersive experiences. From interactive product catalogs that come to life before our eyes to engaging AR advertisements, designers are blurring the line between the digital and physical realms. AR empowers brands to offer captivating experiences, where consumers can explore products in a virtual space, try on virtual clothing, or even visualize how furniture fits into their homes. The realm of design is an exhilarating playground of innovation and creativity.
"What's New in Design" showcases a plethora of opportunities and possibilities, from blending digital and physical experiences with AR to advocating for sustainability and inclusivity. As designers, we have the power to shape the world around us, touching lives through visuals that inspire, inform, and provoke thought. So, let us embrace these new horizons, pushing the boundaries of design to craft a more beautiful, accessible, and meaningful tomorrow.

Fun Design Challenges

Design a Logo Using Only Three Colors

Challenge yourself to create a visually striking logo using just three colors. Consider how you can convey the essence of a brand or concept with a limited color palette.

Create an Illustration with a Given Word

Choose a random word (e.g., "adventure," "serenity," or "bloom") and use it as inspiration to create an illustration that reflects its meaning or emotion.

Design a Business Card for a Fictional Company

Invent a fictional company and design an eye-catching business card that represents its brand identity.

Create a Typography Poster

Design an artistic typography poster with a meaningful quote or phrase that inspires you.
